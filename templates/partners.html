Partners

At Quant, we understand that achieving excellence in AI-driven solutions requires a robust ecosystem of partners. Our global network of partners is essential to our success, enabling us to deliver innovative, high-impact solutions that drive meaningful results for our clients across various industries and regions. We are proud to collaborate with a diverse range of technology, reseller, and implementation partners who share our commitment to delivering differentiated outcomes.

Technology / Reseller Partners

Quant collaborates with a select group of technology and reseller partners who play a critical role in bringing our advanced AI solutions to businesses around the world. These partners are strategically located across three continents—North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific—ensuring that we can meet the needs of our clients wherever they operate.

⚡

Technology Partners

Our technology partners provide the foundational infrastructure and cutting-edge tools that power Quant's AI solutions, ensuring they are robust, scalable, and at the forefront of innovation.

⚡

Resellers Partners

Our reseller partners, on the other hand, are trusted business agents who understand the unique needs of their local markets. They are experienced in identifying and addressing client requirements, providing valuable insights and connections.

Implementation Partners

To ensure the seamless deployment and integration of our AI solutions, Quant works closely with a network of specialized implementation partners. These partners bring deep expertise and industry knowledge, allowing us to deliver customized solutions that align perfectly with our clients' needs.

⚡

System Integrators

Our system integrator partners are experts in connecting Quant's AI solutions with clients' existing IT infrastructure. They ensure smooth and efficient integration, minimizing disruption and maximizing the value of our solutions. With their help, our clients can seamlessly incorporate AI into their operations, enhancing performance and achieving their business goals.

⚡

Service Providers

Quant's service provider partners offer essential managed services and ongoing support, ensuring that our AI solutions continue to deliver value over time. These partners help our clients optimize their AI deployments, providing everything from routine maintenance to advanced analytics services that enhance decision-making and operational efficiency.

⚡

OEMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers)

Our partnerships with OEMs allow us to embed Quant's AI technology directly into hardware and equipment, creating intelligent, automated systems that enhance operational efficiency and customer experiences. These OEM partners help us bring our AI solutions to life in various forms, from smart devices to industrial machinery.

⚡

Partner Affiliates

Quant's network of partner affiliates includes industry experts, consultants, and niche solution providers who offer specialized knowledge and services. These affiliates contribute to the customization and localization of our AI solutions, ensuring they meet the unique requirements of different industries and markets. Whether it's healthcare, finance, manufacturing, or retail, our partner affiliates help us deliver AI solutions that are precisely tailored to the needs of each sector.
